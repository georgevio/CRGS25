Welcome to the "Cyber-Resilient Gateway Systems: Bridging Safety, Security, and Real-Time Operations" Workshop 2025

Held in the vibrant city of Naples, Italy, as part of the DSN 2025 conference. This workshop explores the latest advancements in secure and real-time smart gateways powered by Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS). It delves into the critical role these gateways play in ensuring safe, secure, and efficient communication between devices and (edge) cloud services via smart applications (e.g., AI), in sectors like automotive, healthcare, and industrial automation.
